UPLOAD


STEP 1: Choose Prediction >> Upload.

STEP 2: Choose the image file from local storage(.png, .jpg, .jpeg).

STEP 3: Select Predict button.

To select another image click Clear or Reload the page.


DRAW


STEP 1: Choose Prediction >> Draw.

STEP 2: Draw the Digit in the canvas.

STEP 3: Select Predict button.

To draw another image click Clear or Reload the page.
